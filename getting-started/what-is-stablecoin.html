What is a Stablecoin?

To begin with, there are different types of cryptocurrencies. Other than Bitcoin, you have altcoins, stablecoins, utility tokens, privacy coins, DeFi tokens, and NFTs. An emerging type would be CBDCs (Central Bank Digital Currency).

So what exactly are stablecoins? As the name suggests, they're "stable" as they're often pegged to a particular asset (though this may not be the case for algorithmic stablecoins).

The biggest value of stablecoins is that they offer a more predictable store of value and medium of exchange. This makes it easier to facilitate faster and cheaper cross-border transactions, provide a stable unit of account for DeFi applications, enabling crypto users to quickly move in and out of positions without converting to fiat, etc.
