Types of Stablecoins

Fiat-backed stablecoins

These are pegged 1:1 to fiat currencies, most commonly the US dollar. For each unit of the stablecoin, an equivalent amount of fiat currency is held in reserve.

Examples:

  • - USDT
  • - USDC

Crypto-backed stablecoins

These stablecoins are collateralized by other cryptocurrencies such as ETH. Due to the volatility of crypto assets, they often require over-collateralization (value of collateral > value of loan).

Example:

  • - USDS/DAI
  • - LUSD

Algorithmic stablecoins

These rely mostly on market mechanisms rather than asset reserves and often operate under-collateralized.

Examples:

  • - FRAX
  • - USDe

Commodity-backed stablecoins

This fourth category is pegged to the value of physical assets like precious metals.

Examples:

  • PAXG
  • XAUt (Tether gold)
